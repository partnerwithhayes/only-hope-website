Privacy Policy

Only Hope Family Assets Group respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information submitted through our website, forms, phone calls, emails, and text communications.

Information We Collect

We may collect your name, email address, phone number, property-related information, and any details you choose to provide when contacting us.

How We Use Information

We use information to respond to inquiries, provide requested services, send appointment reminders, provide service updates, and communicate about options that may be available to you.

SMS Communications

If you provide consent, we may send SMS messages related to marketing promotions or non-marketing notifications, depending on the consent option selected. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out and HELP for help.

No Mobile Information Sharing

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. All text messaging originator opt-in data and consent information will remain confidential and will not be shared with any third parties.

Opt-Out

You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message received from us.
