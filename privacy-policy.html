Privacy Policy

At Only Hope Holdings LLC DBA Only Hope Family Assets Group (“Company,” “we,” “our,” or “us”), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, protect, and disclose your information when you visit our website, submit forms, communicate with us, use our services, or interact with our social media platforms.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information including your name, phone number, email address, mailing address, property-related information, IP address, browser/device information, and any information voluntarily submitted through website forms, phone calls, text messages, emails, social media interactions, consultations, or other communication methods.

How We Use Your Information

We may use your information to:

  • Respond to inquiries and provide requested services
  • Contact you regarding consultations, appointments, updates, and support
  • Provide customer service and account communications
  • Send SMS/text messages, emails, and AI-generated voice communications where consent has been provided or where permitted by law
  • Improve our website, services, communications, and customer experience
  • Maintain compliance, legal, and business records
  • Communicate with users through our social media pages and platforms

SMS, AI Voice, and Communication Consent

By providing your phone number and selecting the applicable optional SMS consent checkbox on our contact form, you consent to receive the type of SMS communications selected from Only Hope Holdings LLC DBA Only Hope Family Assets Group.

Marketing SMS messages may include promotional information, service offers, follow-up messages, educational content, and business-related marketing communications.

Non-marketing SMS messages may include customer support messages, appointment reminders, service updates, requested information, account notifications, document reminders, and other service-related communications.

Message frequency may vary. Message and data rates may apply. Consent is not a condition of purchase or service. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to text messages or contacting us directly.

For assistance, reply HELP to any SMS message or contact us directly at (860) 499-1077 or info@onlyhopefamilyassets.com.

Mobile Messaging Privacy

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

All text messaging originator opt-in data and consent information will remain confidential and will not be shared with any third parties, except as required by law or as necessary to facilitate messaging services through trusted telecommunications providers.
