Frequently asked questions

Answers to common questions

How do I know if funds may be owed to me?

If your situation involves a foreclosure, tax sale, inherited property issue, equity concern, or another property-related matter, there may be funds tied to the case. We review available details and help determine whether a next step makes sense.

Do you charge upfront?

Our services are offered with no upfront cost. Specific terms are discussed before engagement so you understand how the process works before moving forward.

Do you guarantee results?

No. Every matter is different, and outcomes depend on the facts, documentation, county procedures, and applicable requirements. We never promise guaranteed results.

Are you a law firm?

No. Only Hope Family Assets Group is not a law firm and does not provide legal advice. We provide professional recovery support and may coordinate with attorneys experienced in surplus recovery matters when legal assistance is appropriate.
